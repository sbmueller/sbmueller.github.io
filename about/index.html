About me.

I’m an Electrical Engineer (M.Sc.) who graduated in communications technology.

During my EE studies I gained excellent theoretical knowledge of communications engineering and signal processing. During extracurricular activities, I successfully took part in several competitions dealing with transmission, reception and parameter estimation of diverse waveforms using GNU Radio and MATLAB. Additionally, the Google Summer of Code program 2016 has provided me with plenty of hands-on experience in C++ and Python while developing a signal analysis toolbox for GNU Radio.

I easily get enthused by tasks about wireless communications, radio signal processing and software engineering and am constantly looking for new challenges.
